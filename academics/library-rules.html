Library Rules

Academics > Library Rules
  • Every student will be issued one non-transferable library card.
  • Books are issued and collected during library periods only. A book is issued for one week and may be reissued for another one week only.
  • A duplicate library card will cost ₹25.
  • Students are expected to maintain absolute silence in the library. All students are required to read books in the library.
  • The loss or damage of books will incur a fine amounting to the double cost of the book.
