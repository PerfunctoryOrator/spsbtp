Admission Policy

Admissions > Admission Policy
  • Although a minority institution, we do admit children of diverse castes and creeds.
  • Children under three years six months or over four years six months will not be admitted to L.K.G. For higher classes, an additional year shall be added for each class.
  • Pupils will not be admitted to classes II and above without a Transfer Certificate from the school they last attended.
  • Students from Rajasthan seeking admission for classes VI and above must submit their Transfer Certificates, signed by the relevant education authorities.
  • Transfer Certificates of pupils coming from outside Rajasthan must be countersigned by proper authorities, regardless of the class into which admission is sought.
  • A pupil seeking admission must provide the date of birth certificate issued by the municipal authorities or a Baptism Certificate, along with the transfer certificate.
  • Admission will be granted based on the Entrance Test and against the availability of seats.
  • Fees will be charged from the commencement of the academic session regardless of the time of admission.
  • Students will not be admitted to any class higher than that for which the Transfer Certificate shows their qualification, nor will they be granted out-of-turn promotions.
