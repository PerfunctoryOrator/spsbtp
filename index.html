St. Peter’s School, Bharatpur, established in 1970 under the aegis of the Archdiocese of Agra, is dedicated to education and human development. Initially, the school operated from a rented building in Ranjeet Nagar, starting with just nine students, one teacher, and two priests who laid the foundation for St. Peter’s School in Bharatpur. Under the leadership of Reverent Father Thomas Paramundayil, who assumed administration in 1975, the school has carved out a significant place for itself in Rajasthan’s educational landscape.

For more information, see Our History.
Our Mission
We strive to provide High-Quality Education for children in a Safe, Respectful and Inclusive Environment that builds a Foundation for Life-Long Learning.
Our Vision
We envision and continuously strive for a community where All Children Feel Loved, Respected and Encouraged to develop to their Fullest Potential.
Fr. Shiju Pallippadan, the Principal of St. Peter’s Sr. Sec. School, Bharatpur, was born on 2 September, 1975. He joined the Archdiocese of Agra on 26 April, 2003 and has been serving as the Principal of this school since 2022.
